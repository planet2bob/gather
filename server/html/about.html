gather

Gather is a social messaging aggregation platform. We combine social messaging services for users to send and receive messages from their Slack, WeChat, and Skype accounts all at once. No more notifications on five separate apps, no more needing to tediously log in to multiple messaging systems. Gather combines everything into one to improve your online experience.

The creators of Gather aim to increase the convenience of social messaging. This project was created by students of an independent study class. We would really apreciate your feedback! Please feel free contact us at wuj@bishops.com.
